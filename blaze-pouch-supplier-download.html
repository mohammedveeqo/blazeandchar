03

Before you hit send — quick checklist

  • Save both HTML files as PDF (open each, Cmd+P, "Save as PDF"). Attach the PDFs, not the .html files.
  • In the supplier brief PDF, fill in the [DATE], [NAME], and [email] placeholders on page 1.
  • Confirm the pouch dimensions (140 × 220 × 70 mm, 60g fill) match what you want. Supplier will quote from that number.
  • Make sure your email address is a real one you check — replies from suppliers often come quickly and they'll want a call.
  • If sending to multiple suppliers, send identical files so the quotes are comparable. Don't let one supplier's push change your spec for others.
  • Ask for a physical press-proof before the full run. Never approve artwork for print based on a screen preview only.

Three UK suppliers to try first

ePac Flexible Packaging
epacflexibles.com · Silverstone / Manchester
Digital print, low MOQ (~1000), fast lead time. Best for pre-launch / small batches.
Swiss Pac UK
swisspac.co.uk · Surrey-based, ships UK-wide
Wide material range including recyclable mono-material. Good for mid-volume (2500+).
Morrisette Paper & Packaging
morrisettepaper.com · UK reseller, EU printing
Longer lead times but premium finishes (spot-UV, matte lam). Good for launch-quality run.
Mondi Group
mondigroup.com · European, multi-site
Large, professional, recyclable specialists. Probably 5000+ MOQ. Right for scale-up, not launch.
