The short version.

For quote

We're launching a small-batch UK jerky brand (first SKUs: Tandoori Original + Masala Warm & Rich) and need 2,000 printed flat pillow pouches for our pre-launch run — 1,000 per SKU. This document contains the full spec, visual reference, colour system, typography, and what we need back from you.

01

Pouch specification

Pouch typeFlat pillow pouch (3-side-seal) — flat top and bottom heat seals, no bottom gusset. Standard jerky-category format (Jack Link's, Kings, Wild West etc). Works on peg-hook displays and lies flat in shipping.
Net fill weight60 g of hand-cut dried jerky strips. Pouch capacity should comfortably accommodate this plus headspace.
Dimensions (approx.)130 mm W × 185 mm H (flat-measure, no gusset). Happy to use your nearest standard tooling within ±5 mm if it avoids custom-die cost. Include a 6 mm round hang hole centered 6 mm below the top edge for peg-hook retail display.
MaterialRecyclable mono-material (PE-based), food-grade, OPR/HDPE laminate. Must support UK widely-recyclable claim. Fallback: metallised PET/PE laminate if mono-material is cost-prohibitive at 2,000 MOQ.
FinishMatte laminate for the pouch body, with spot-gloss UV on the wordmark and ampersand only (≈ 6 cm² total per pouch).
WindowClear product-reveal window, horizontal slit, approx 75 mm × 30 mm, positioned in the lower-middle section of the front panel so product fill is always visible. See visual reference for exact placement.
ClosureZipper reseal along the top, positioned below the heat-seal. Tear-notch (V-cut) on one edge above the zipper for consumer first-open. Consumer tears the top strip off, then reseals via zipper.
Valve / degassingNot required. Dried product — no off-gassing.
Print processRotogravure or digital flexo. 4-colour process (CMYK) with one spot Pantone for the accent red (brand-critical — see Colour spec). Black foot bar printed solid, not process black.
SKUsTwo: Tandoori Original (front+back artwork, red stock) and Masala Warm & Rich (front+back artwork, honey-gold stock). Same pouch dimensions, same material, same finish — different front/back artwork per SKU.
Quantity — first run1,000 per SKU × 2 SKUs = 2,000 pouches total. Please also quote 2,500 and 5,000 per SKU so we can compare.
Lead timePlease quote first-order lead time from final print-ready artwork sign-off, and re-order lead time from repeat order date.
CertificationsFood-contact safe (required). BRCGS or equivalent preferred. We are not HMC-certified yet — no halal-cert logo needs to be printed by you.
Not in this document: print-ready artwork (CMYK PDFs with bleed and crop marks) will be supplied by our print-designer once we have your quote and spec-match confirmed. This document is for your quote and sample-check only.
