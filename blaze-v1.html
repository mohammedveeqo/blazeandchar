The Story

Slow-marinated.

We marinate premium halal beef in a handcrafted tandoori spice blend with tamarind, Kashmiri chilli, and smoked paprika — then slow-dehydrate every batch for bold, clean flavour. No soy. No preservatives. Nothing like it exists in the UK.
