How to read this — what to look for

You're comparing twelve different answers to the same question: what should the front of a premium hand-made jerky pouch look like?

Key things to weigh as you compare:

  • Shelf read at 2 metres — squint at each card. Which ones are clearly beef jerky? Which read as 'premium snack' but don't tell you what's in the bag?
  • Protein as a hero stat — every concept (except current) promotes protein to a visible stamp, callout, or badge. Does it feel like the right weight?
  • Graphic vs type — some concepts (01, 12) rely entirely on typography. Some (06, 08, 09) hero an illustration. Which direction fits the brand voice you have in your head?
  • Heritage signalling — Wales is present in all concepts but handled differently. Concept 06 makes it mythical (dragon), concept 10 makes it landscape, concept 11 makes it rustic. Which is right for you?
  • Print complexity — simpler concepts (01, 12) will print cheaper and be easier to reproduce on any supplier's kit. More detailed ones (02, 08, 10, 11) look richer but cost more per bag.

Tell me the number you want (or two you're torn between) and I'll develop it into the canonical — same treatment the current Chilli & Honey got, but with the new hero direction baked in.
