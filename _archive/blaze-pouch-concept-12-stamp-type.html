Concept 12 · Rubber-stamp typography · Bold type

The rubber stamp — type as the whole hero

Everything is typography inside a thick rubber-stamp frame. No illustration at all — the word Tandoori (or Masala) is the hero. Bold, confident, cheaper to print (no colour halftones), reads from across the room. Lineage: Ember's rubber-stamped brand block, Brat's restaurant signage, Grind coffee packs.
