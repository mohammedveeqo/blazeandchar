How to choose

A (editorial tan) — quieter, closer to how your website looks. Feels like a premium deli counter. Best if you're going to sell alongside speciality food (cheese shops, wine merchants, posh farm shops).

B (butcher kraft) — warmer, louder. Reads as meat product from across a shelf. The double-rule borders, heavier wordmark and chunky "24g" medal work harder when the pouch has to compete with Peperami, Biltong Co, Jack Link's etc. Best if you're pitching to mainstream grocers / petrol forecourts / gyms.

Both use the same copy, same ingredients, same legal wording, same Welsh stamp — swap in either for the print-ready artwork. Let me know which one and I'll build its back panel to match.
