The full brand kit as it stands today. Seven logo SVGs (three monogram variants + four full-logo variants), the wordmark-only file, the colour palette, and the type stack. All files are ready to drop into invoices, decks, emails, or print.

Monogram · Primary
The & monogram

The most distinctive glyph in the wordmark promoted to a standalone brand mark. Italic Fraunces 400, SOFT 20, WONK 1, tandoori red on transparent.

03

Pouch master — the canonical artwork

The single source of truth for the pouch design. Every pair variant and colour-way below is generated from this file by overriding palette tokens — structure, copy, legal, and layout all flow from here.

Pouch · Canonical master (v7 final)

Tandoori + Masala — front & back

60g stand-up pouch, warm tan stock. Front: wordmark, hero name, BEEF JERKY category line, window, nutrition creds row, heat scale, three certs (hand-cut / 36g protein / Wales). Back: story, ingredients, nutrition panel, storage, batch + best-before, maker block, barcode placeholder, halal footer.

UK-labelling compliant (mustard allergen emphasis, topside not silverside, "born/reared/slaughtered UK" provenance language). The Wales silhouette is filled solid green via your clean single-path SVG.
